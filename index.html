Ride a Bicycle!


Bicycles were introduced in the 19th century in Europe and as of 2003,
more than 1 billion have been produced worldwide,
twice as many as the number of automobiles that have been produced.
They are the principal means of transportation in many regions.
They also provide a popular form of recreation,
and have been adapted for use as children's toys, general fitness,
military and police applications, courier services, and bicycle racing.

Find out more about bicycles!

There are many types of bicycles:

  • road bicycles
  • folding bicycles
  • mountain bicycles
  • cruiser bicycles
